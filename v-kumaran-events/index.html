Our Event Process

How we bring your dream celebrations to life

1

Step 1: Consultation

We discuss your thoughts, themes, estimated guest list, budget, and specific custom requirements.

2

Step 2: Planning

We formulate layout schedules, structure logistics, map timelines, and select raw materials.

3

Step 3: Design & Decoration

We render draft color layouts, customized columns, structural trims, and compile floral designs.

4

Step 4: Event Execution

On-site staging setup, sound testing, lighting configurations, guest reception desks, and operations.

5

Step 5: Celebration Success

A flawless event wraps up successfully, creating happy moments and everlasting memories.
